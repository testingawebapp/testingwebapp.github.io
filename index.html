About TD Studio

The businesses aim was to implement strategies to make it easy for people around the community to have access to the internet which was very beneficial to withstand situations like Covid-19 whereby people were restricted from going to social places e.g. Work, Gym, Shops, etc.

Hence the design studio came in handy. If people have 4th Industrial knowledge it will be easy for everyone to use internet as a tool of life. The business will also boost entrepreneurship which is not taught on the Basic Education.
